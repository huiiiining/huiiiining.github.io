What is Cyberbullying?
Harassing or humiliating someone via digital platforms. Unlike traditional bullying, it can reach huge audiences 24/7.

The Result:
By reporting, the school identified the bullies. They were reprimanded, and the content was flagged and removed from SocialSnap.
