Reporting Was the Right Choice

What is Cyberbullying?
It is the use of digital platforms to harass, threaten, or humiliate someone. Unlike traditional bullying, it can happen 24/7 and reach a huge audience instantly.

The Result:
Because Olivia reported the incident, the school administration was able to identify the students spreading the video. They were reprimanded according to school policy, and the harmful content was flagged and removed from SocialSnap.

Olivia didn't have to face this alone. By speaking up, she regained control of her digital life.
